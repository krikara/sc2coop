Game Core

  • After Warpgate Research completion, Gateways will automatically transform into Warpgates, even if a unit is in production. That unit's supply and cost is not refunded. Reverting to Gateway will complete production.
  • Aura effects apply with units even if they are inside transports
  • Beam Weapons are unaffected by attack speed buffs.
  • Units with the "Frenzied" passive can get stunned with Stank's Headbutt, Hunterling's Leap Attack and stun abilities from Void Shards and Slivers
  • Players can destroy their initial structures and rebuild them elsewhere on certain missions and stop mutator units such as Propagators from being given a move command to their base
  • Players can play at 180 mastery points by using an arcade exploit
  • Prestiging a commander while the game countdown is active adds experience to the level 1 commander while the player plays at mastery level
  • Score screen displays jumping units (e.g. Raptor-strain Zerglings) as units made

Amon

  • Attack Waves take units from the map if they are killed too quickly
  • Attack waves with only air units can take unexpected paths to player's bases
  • Brutal+ Mutations can be stacked with the Weekly Mutation if you retry Brutal+ on the Weekly Mutation tab
  • Casting a Fear debuff on an entire attack wave will cause units in the wave to lose their attack commands after Fear wears off
  • Disruptor's Purification Novas are not cancelled if lifted by Phoenixes
  • Air units can be stuck in a state between Air and Ground if they are ensnared (e.g. from Stukov's Infested Diamondbacks) and then get teleported via triggers (e.g. on Oblivion Express)
  • Ghosts and Wraiths can use Cloak at the start of the mission, but these upgrades are provided at 15:00
  • Hybrid Destroyer's Graviton Prison is not marked as a Stasis ability
  • Raven Seeker Missiles will hit Interceptors if it was the target even if the Interceptor is already inside the Carrier before it hits

Abathur

  • Base
    • Swarm Host's Spawn Locust cooldown is based on attack speed.
    • Swarm Host's Spawn Locust cooldown buffed by a Psionic Devouring One (P1) Dehaka reduces the cooldown of the ability, but will not be autocasted.
    • Symbiotes can attack when unit is in transport
    • Symbiotes gets Stetmann's H.U.G.S. Overload shield despite being invulnerable
    • Toxic Nests can be placed on top of Swann Flaming Betties, making them unable to unburrow and attack
  • Mutators
    • Black Death damage to self is counted for Life Leech bonus provided by Biomass
    • Brutalisks can be Feared, despite having the Frenzied passive
    • Double Edged damage to self is counted for Life Leech bonus provided by Biomass
    • Ravagers are pulled by Fatal Attraction while burrowed

    • Havocs can provide buffs from inside War Prisms
    • If you use /dance with Wrathwalkers, they lose the ability to fire while moving if they move right after dancing
    • Mothership Thermal Lance can auto target Thrasher projectiles and cloaked units if there is at least 1 visible target in its autocast range
    • Slayer Phasing Armor neither cancels Hybrid Destroyer's Graviton Prison nor prevents any damage
    • Structure Overcharge still fires from structures in stasis by a Hybrid Nemesis
  • Mutators
    • Alarak's Force Fields do not block Zagara's Baneling Barrage
    • Structure Overcharge can target units with Polarity even though it cannot damage them

    • The lava surge damage on The Vermillion Problem ignores Guardian Shell
  • Mutators
    • Black Death damage ignores Guardian Shell
    • Abilities casted by Power Overwhelming ignore Guardian Shell invulnerability and still damage the unit

Dehaka

  • Base
    • Broodbrother (P3) respawn timer is only based on Dehaka's level

    • Devouring One (P1) Dehaka devours can heal mission objectives like Aurana on Malwarfare, Harvesting Bots on Mist Opportunities, and Miner Evacuation Ships

    • The range buff that units get when Devouring One (P1) Dehaka devours an air unit is 15 seconds and is unaffected by masteries or Devour level
    • Thorns from Tyrannozors and Dakrun can be blocked by Point Defense Drones
    • When a Dehaka Brother gains the Deadly Reach upgrade with Broodbrother (P3), the other brother gets the upgrade too, regardless of level
    • When Zweihaka spawns, he gets auto-rallied to different locations
  • Mutators
    • Dakrun and Tyrannozors are instantly killed when Double-Edged is active

    • Widow Mines are pulled by Fatal Attraction while burrowed
    • Widow Mines lock on and fire at units in Polarity

Karax

  • Mutators

    • Chokers make Torrasque Ultralisk unable to die while resurrection is available
    • Folly of Man (P2) lightning strikes can hit units under Polarity, stunning them, but not damaging them
    • Kerrigan can get pulled by Fatal Attraction while burrowed, moving her hitbox, but not the model
    • Temporal Field makes Torrasque Ultralisk unable to die while resurrection is available

Mengsk

  • Base
    • Aegis Guard at Rank 2 will ignore all tags for its Slow debuff
    • Aegis Guard rank 2 splash deals splash damage dependent of main target of attack and not the splash targets themselves
    • Attacks from Bunkers don't draw aggro to Bunkers if they are outside of enemy vision
    • Blackhammer in Overwatch mode hits ground units below targeted air unit
    • Bunkers are immune to Disabling Cloud and Disruption Web
    • Emperor's Shadows do not autocast Labyrinth Cloak when taking damage
    • Laborers can construct structures while carrying crystals on The Vermillion Problem.
    • Principal Proletariat's (P2) Supply cost increase does not count for unlocking "The Best of the Best of the Best, Emperor" achievement
    • Rank 3 Pyrokinetic Immolation from Emperor's Shadow deals 50 damage area of effect when it expires even though the unit has not died
    • Shock Divison + Intercessor deals different damages at different ranks to what is listed on the weapon stat
    • Troopers inside bunkers can pick up dropped weapons
  • Mutators
    • Bunkers do not catch fire with Scorched Earth at melee range

Nova

  • Base
    • Infiltration Specialist (P3) Nova gets decloaked when firing or sniping if in Vorazun's Dark Pylon range

    • Railgun Turrets do not deal damage when they attack a target at maximum range.
