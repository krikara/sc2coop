Summary

When you complete missions (either completely or partially), you earn mission experience. This experience goes towards levelling. There are a total of three phases in levelling. These phases are:

  1. Commander Levels: Levels range from 1-15. As you level a commander, you unlock upgrades that make the commander stronger. Commander levels are not shared between commanders.
  2. Mastery Levels: Levels range from 0-90. As you level your mastery, you are awarded with mastery points that you can put towards each commander. Mastery levels are shared between commanders. Every mastery level gives you one mastery point to put towards each commander you have. These mastery points make your commanders much more powerful and improve upon their passive bonuses or abilities. At max level, you will get a total of 90 Mastery Points to distribute into three different power sets, 30 points each. Each power set requires you to pick one of two special perks available, unique to the commander.
  3. Ascension Levels: Levels range from 91-1000. They do not award anything except for some cosmetic rewards. Ascension levels are shared between commanders.

When a commander reaches level 15 (requiring a total of 1,045,000 experience), a player may choose to Prestige them. This resets the commander back down to level 1 and unlocks a new selectable talent. A player may Prestige a maximum of three times, unlocking three talents.

  • Bonus Completion: 2,000 EXP
