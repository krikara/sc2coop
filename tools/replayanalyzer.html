Co-op Replay Analyzer

Analyze Your Replays

Upload your replay file below to see statistics regarding to your gameplay. Please note:

  • Due to the fact that Replay files are optimized for Ladder games, certain information may not be accurate or available. For example, Resource count, supplies and mining rates are usually accurate whereas Tech Spending and army resource values and lost usually contains inaccurate data as some upgrades and units are not counted. This includes data in the "Economy Minerals Lost" tile.
  • Fluctuations in resource gain may occur, as data samples are collected once every 10 seconds.
  • This parser only works on replay files played using the Co-op game mode. Arcade games and Ladder Games are not supported.
  • Calldown efficiency for Energy/Mandate-based calldowns will not be displayed.
  • Extracting Ability information (such as Kerrigan's Immobilization Wave, Alarak's Structure Overcharge) is extremely limited. Any issues will be displayed in the "Notes" section.
  • Mutator detection is only guaranteed for Custom mutation games. Brutal+ difficulty and Weekly Mutation mutators may not be detected.
